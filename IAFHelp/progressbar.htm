Progress bar
   

To insert new progress bar on the form, click button Progress in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Progress. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the top left corner of the progress bar. Drag to the right and bottom and release the button. New progress bar is placed on the form.

To change progress bar properties double click on the progress bar

-or-

select the progress bar, right click to display context menu and choose Properties.

How run-time engine processes the progress bar?

There are two methods to display progress of a lengthy operation. The first, Setrange defines lower and upper limits of the range. After that, we repeatedly call Setpos to indicate current position.
