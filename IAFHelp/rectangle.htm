Rectangle
   

To insert new rectangle on the form, click button Rectangle in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Rectangle. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the top left corner of the rectangle. Drag to the right and bottom and release the button. New rectangle is placed on the form.

Create a square

Click button Rectangle in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Rectangle. Press Shift. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.

Create rectangle with maximal width

Click button Rectangle in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Rectangle. Press Ctrl. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.


To change rectangle properties double click on the rectangle edge

-or-

select the rectangle, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.
