Absolute positions
   

Normally, a lineset is printed after the preceding lineset. Iaf enables you to print a lineset in the same position on each page.

In Lineset column you see all linesets in the report.

In Position column, you have to enter the absolute position in millimeters.

Example: the invoice form. The invoice amount is always printed at the bottom of the page.

If you want to reserve the space where this lineset is to be printed, even when the lineset is not printed on every page, set 'Overflow position' to absolute_position - 1. See 'Define report dimensions' dialog.

Note

Page header is always printed on the top of the page and does not make sense to define the absolute position for it.
