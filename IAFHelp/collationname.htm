Collation names

            Collation name has the format

            Language[_CI] where _CI means case-insensitive.

            Examples:
                        English                         : English alphabet, case-sensitive
                        English_CI                    : English alphabet, case-insensitive
                        Serbian_Cyrillic
                        Serbian_Cyrillic_CI
                        Serbian_Latin
                        Serbian_Latin_CI
