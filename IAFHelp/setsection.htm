Setsection
   

This method creates a section of a grid. The section is continuous group of lines that can be hidden.

Format

            Setsection (line_start, line_end)


Parameters


·     line_start - start of the section, numeric value, integer. The first line is 1.

·     line_end - end of the section, numeric value, integer.


Applied To

            Grid.

Note

            
Examples

  
      Table_1.Setsection(5,20)
      Table_1.Setsection($line_from,$line_end)
