NEXT
   

This statement starts next iteration of immediately innermost loop (DO_WHILE , READ_ALL or SELECT_ALL). All the statements between NEXT and end of loop will be skipped.

Format

            NEXT

Notes

            If the NEXT is used in Additional view actions outside of a loop, next record from the input record stream will be read.

Example

  
      Select_all tbl
            If tbl.fld1 = 100
                  Next
            End_if
            ...
      End_read


      counter_1 = 0
      do_while counter_1 < 10
            add 1 to counter_1
            if counter_1 = 6 or counter_1 = 8
                  Next
            end_if
            ...
      end_do
