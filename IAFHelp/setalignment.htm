Setalignment
   

This method sets text alignment.

Format 1
  
 Setalignment (alignment)
 Sets alignment of an object

Format 2
  
 Setalignment (line, column, alignment)
 Sets alignment of a grid cell

Parameters


·    alignment - alpha variable, alignment in the form
                                    HORIZONTAL = {CENTER | LEFT | RIGHT} or
                                    VERTICAL = {CENTER | TOP | BOTTOM}

                        HORIZONTAL and VERTICAL can be abbreviated.

·     line - grid line, numeric value. The lines are counted from 1.To specify current line, we can use the internal variable CURRENT_LINE or star (*).

·     column - grid column, numeric value. The columns are counted from 1.

Notes

            To align entire grid line, use ENTIRE_LINE constant in the form

                        table_1.Setalignment(line,ENTIRE_LINE,alignment)

            To align entire grid column, use ENTIRE_COLUMN constant in the form

                        table_1.Setalignment(ENTIRE_COLUMN,column,alignment)


Examples

  
      label_1.Setalignment('HOR=LEFT')    !! horiz. alignment=left
      label_1.Setalignment('HOR=CENTER')  !! horiz. alignment=center
      label_1.Setalignment('HOR=RIGHT')   !! horiz. alignment=right
      label_1.Setalignment('VERT=CENTER') !! vert. alignment=center

      table_1.Setalignment(ENTIRE_COLUMN,1,'HOR=RIGHT')
