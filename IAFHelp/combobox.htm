Combo Box
   

We can consider combo box as a destination field which stores one of many values: 0 or 1 or 2 or 3, "A" or "B" or "C" or ...  See also Radio button. To select many items at the same time, use Data selector.


To insert new combobox on the form, click button Combobox in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Combobox. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click.
Definition dialog appears on the screen.

New combobox is placed on the form.

Iaf supports drop-down list style: list-box portion is visible when dropped down and user cannot type any character in edit-box portion

To change combobox properties double click on the combobox

-or-

select the combobox, right click to display context menu and choose Properties.
