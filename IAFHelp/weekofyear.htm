WEEK_OF_YEAR
   

This function returns week of a year. According to ISO 8601, week 01 of a year is the first week that has the Thursday in that year, which is equivalent to the week that contains the fourth day of January.

Format

            WEEK_OF_YEAR(date_exp)

Return Value Type

            Numeric.

            If result is 0,  date_exp is zero_date or NULL.

            If result is -1 and date_exp <= 3-jan-YYYY, date_exp belongs to the last week of the previous year.
            If result is -1 and date_exp >= 29-dec-YYYY, date_exp belongs to the first week of the next year.

            Otherwise, the result is 1 to 53.

Parameters


·     date_exp - input parameter, date or datetime.

Notes

Week 01 may contain days from the previous year.
The last week of a year may contain days from the next year.

The first week of the year 2004 lasts from 29-dec-2003 to 4-jan-2004.
The first week of the year 2005 lasts from 3-jan-2005 to 9-jan-2005.
The first week of the year 2006 lasts from 2-jan-2006 to 8-jan-2006.
The first week of the year 2007 lasts from 1-jan-2007 to 7-jan-2007.

Examples

  
      $week = week_of_year("1-jan-2006")  !! result is -1
      $week = week_of_year("2-jan-2006")  !! result is 1
      $week = week_of_year("10-jan-2006") !! result is 2
