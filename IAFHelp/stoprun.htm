STOP_RUN
   

This statement stops program.

Format

            STOP_RUN [/SYSTEM]

Qualifier

            /SYSTEM   ‘’jumps’’ over ending actions.

Note

            Control is transfered to ending actions of the program, executes ending actions and stops the program. If /SYSTEM qualifier is used, program control ‘’jumps’’ over ending actions and goes directly to an operating system.

Example

  
      STOP_RUN
