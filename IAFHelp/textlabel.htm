Text (label)
   

To insert new text on the form, click button Text in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Text.

Move the cursor to the desired position, then click. A small single-line edit box appears on the screen. Enter the text and press Enter or click outside the edit box.

-or-

Move the cursor to the desired position, then click and select (rubber-band) an arbitrary rectangular area enabling multiline text label. Enter the text and press Enter or click outside the edit box.


To change text properties double click on the text

-or-

select the text, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.

Note

If the symbol IAFUNICODE has value 1, all text labels will be saved in Unicode format.
