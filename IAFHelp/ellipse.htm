Ellipse
   

To insert new ellipse on the form, click button Ellipse in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Ellipse. Move the cursor to the desired position, then click. Drag to the right and bottom and release the button. New ellipse is placed on the form.

Create a circle

Click button Ellipse in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Ellipse. Press Shift. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.

Create ellipse with maximal width

Click button Ellipse in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Ellipse. Press Ctrl. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.


To change ellipse properties double click on the ellipse

-or-

select the ellipse, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.
