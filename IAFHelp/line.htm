Line
   

To insert new line on the form, click button Line in toolbox or choose Objects/Line. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the beginning of the line. Drag to the desired end of the line and release the button. New line is placed on the form.

Create horizontal line

Click button Line in toolbox or choose Objects/Line. Press Shift. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the beginning of the line. Drag to the right or left, release the mouse button and release the shift key.

Create vertical line

Click button Line in toolbox or choose Objects/Line. Press Shift. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the beginning of the line. Drag to the top or bottom, release the mouse button and release the shift key.

Create maximal horizontal line

Click button Line in toolbox or choose Objects/Line. Press Ctrl. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click.


To change line properties (style, width, color) double click on the line

-or-

select the line, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.
