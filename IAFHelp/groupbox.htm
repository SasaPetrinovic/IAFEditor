Group box
   

The group box is a rectangle with some text located on the top edge.

 

To insert new group box on the form, click button Group box in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/Group box. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the top left corner of the group box. Drag to the right and bottom and release the button. New group box with default text "Group box" is placed on the form.

To change group box properties double click on the frame edge

-or-

select the group box, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.
