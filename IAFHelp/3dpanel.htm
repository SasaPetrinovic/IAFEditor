3D Panel
   

To insert new panel on the form, click button 3D panel in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/3D panel. Move the cursor to the desired position,  then click. This is the top left corner of the rectangle. Drag to the right and bottom and release the button. New panel is placed on the form.

Create a square panel

Click button 3D panel in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/3D panel. Press Shift. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.

Create panel with maximal width

Click button 3D panel in toolbox or choose menu command Objects/3D panel. Press Ctrl. Move the cursor to the desired position, click and drag to the bottom.


To change panel properties double click on the panel edge

-or-

select the panel, right click to display context menu and choose 'Properties'.
